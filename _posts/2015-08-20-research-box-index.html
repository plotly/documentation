FAQ

Do you do trials?

Research Box trials are $5000 for 30 days and can be credited towards a Research Box license. We charge this trial fee to cover the overhead costs in setting up a private cloud database and training.

Can you help migrate our data?

Yes. Research Box comes with 10 free consulting hours that can be used to to migrate data from different databases, Excel files, Google forms etc to Research Box.

Can you connect to instrumentation?

Yes. Research Box connects to LabView, Arbin machines, SRS instrumentation, and MATLAB. We recommend purchasing a block of 40 consulting hours with Research Box so we can help you connect your instrumentation.

Can you upload images?

Yes. High-resolution SEM, optical microscope, and other image types can be uploaded.
