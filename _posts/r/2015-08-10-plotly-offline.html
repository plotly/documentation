Plotly Offline for RStudio and Shiny¶

NEW: Plotly's R library v2.0 renders Plotly graphs offline in RStudio.


  • Since November 17 2015, Plotly R figures now appear interactively within RStudio's graphics viewer without any calls to Plotly's server by default.
  • Plotly graphics in RStudio sit on top of the HTML widgets framework and use the open source javascript graphing library plotly.js.
  • When you're ready to share, you can publish your Plotly R figures to the Plotly Cloud or to your company's internal Plotly On-Premise server.
    To get started with Plotly offline in RStudio, download Plotly's free R client.
