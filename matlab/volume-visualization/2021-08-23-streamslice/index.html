MATLAB - streamslice

Plot Streamlines in Slice Plane

Load the wind data set, which loads the three-dimensional arrays u, v, w, x, y, and z. Draw streamlines along the slice plane where z = 5.

  load wind
streamslice(x,y,z,u,v,w,[],[],5)
axis tight
fig2plotly()
