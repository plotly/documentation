Dashboards for a new generation of insurance analytics

Plotly is a passport for your data that gives your insurance agents, underwriters, and managers the power to drill into code-level detail in just a few clicks. Our Workspace gives non-technical users the power to build, upload, and design their own interactive visualizations.

Track insurance metrics like customer satisfaction, claim ratios, broker performance with or without code – it’s up to you.

Boston-based car insurance provider Goji saved over one hundred thousand dollars on BI infrastructure by replacing their traditional data warehouse and monolithic, GUI-based data analysis software with Plotly On-Premise.

Thanks to Plotly dashboards, Goji updates its internal reporting in real-time from multiple sources: their call centers, their CRM system and their website. Our dashboards give the Goji data science team the power to visualize and share web-based charts and graphs with colleagues. Plotly’s interactive dashboards are smart, clear and easy for everyone in the office to follow.
