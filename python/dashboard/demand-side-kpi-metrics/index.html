To help new and existing marketplace founders bring their KPIs to life, these four dashboards were created using Plot.ly and Keen.io

Read more about why having KPI dashboards are important.

  • Marketplace Summary Metrics High level metrics to highlight whats happening in the marketplace
  • Supply Side Metrics Metrics highlighting specific trends with the supplier side of the marketplace
  • Demand Side Metrics Metrics highlighting specific trends with the demand side of the marketplace
  • Mashup Metrics Metrics that mash up buyer and supplier side metrics to see if there are any trends that stand out

MoM and YoY Buyer Growth
See how growth in buyer on boarding varies over time. The YoY data may help see some seasonality trends, but not always if the company is young
% of Revenue Generated by Top 20% of Buyers
See how important the top 20% of buyers are to GMV and how this changes over time
Percentage of Buyers with >1 Purchase and % of Buyers with 2nd Purchase in New Category
See cohort activity related to multiple purchases and 2nd purchase in another category
Buyers Acquired and % Acquired from Paid Acquisition
See the new buyers added each month and how your paid acquisition efforts are driving the new additions
Buyer Acquisition Costs (Blended and Paid)
See the difference between acquisition costs for all buyers vs. paid buyer. Next question could be "Are paid buyers more or less valuable to the marketplace based on the trend you see?"
