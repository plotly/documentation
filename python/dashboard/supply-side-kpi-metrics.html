To help new and existing marketplace founders bring their KPIs to life, these four dashboards were created using Plot.ly and Keen.io

Read more about why having KPI dashboards are important.

  • Marketplace Summary Metrics High level metrics to highlight whats happening in the marketplace
  • Supply Side Metrics Metrics highlighting specific trends with the supplier side of the marketplace
  • Demand Side Metrics Metrics highlighting specific trends with the demand side of the marketplace
  • Mashup Metrics Metrics that mash up buyer and supplier side metrics to see if there are any trends that stand out

% of Revenue Generated by Top 20% of Sellers

Total Listings and Listings Growth
See the trend in total listings from suppliers and how the growth in listings is trending over time
Percentage of Sellers Active After 1-Month and 1-Year
See how cohort activity trends over time. Could explore adding 3, 6, and 9-month metrics as well
Average % of Month 1 GMV Generated by Cohort in Month 12
See what cohort activity and retention looks like with this point in time snapshot
Sellers Acquired and % Acquired from Paid Acquisition
See the new sellers added each month and how your paid acquisition efforts are driving the new additions
Sellers Acquisition Costs (Blended and Paid)
See the difference between acquisition costs for all suppliers vs. paid suppliers. Next question could be "Are paid suppliers more or less valuable to the marketplace based on the trend you see?"
