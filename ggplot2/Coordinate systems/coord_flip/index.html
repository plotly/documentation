GGPLOT - coord_flip

Creates Cartesian coordinates with x and y flipped and then convert them with ggplotly.

p <-  
 ggplot(diamonds, aes(cut, price)) +
  geom_boxplot() +
  coord_flip()

plotly::ggplotly(p)

h <- ggplot(diamonds, aes(carat)) +
  geom_histogram()

plotly::ggplotly(h)
